Thinkpads are great

(older) Thinkpads are absolute computing perfection.

  • The keyboard actually makes sense, it's styled like a desktop's keyboard, the buttons are properly sized. Unlike modern laptops, they have a volume button, I repeat, they have a volume button! . F keys don't count shut up
  • Don't prevent you from upgrading and reparing.
  • Linux on Thinkpads work flawlessly.
  • There is an abundance of used Thinkpads for cheap on 2nd hand sites. Craigslist, Gumtree, whatever.

Best models imo are T420, T450, x260, x200 or x60.
